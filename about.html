About The Studio

Innovative spaces, executed with engineering precision.

Anand Designs brings detailed schematic design, structural drafting, and project supervision together for custom residences and commercial developments in Jaipur.

Our Design Philosophy

Architecture should enhance spatial experience and structural logic.

Under the leadership of Principal Architect Sudhir, Anand Designs has earned a solid reputation in Jaipur for creating smart, functional, and visually striking structures. We believe that architecture must solve site complexities, maximize solar design, and respect local guidelines.

From the initial zoning analysis and client sketch sessions to structural calculations and electrical coordination, we deliver thorough and comprehensive project blueprints.

We work closely with structural consultants, municipal inspectors, and skilled site contractors to make sure the final building matches our exact specifications.
